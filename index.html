About

I’m an audio engineer and music producer based in Nashville, TN. I’ve been working in the industry for more than 8 years, in the studio and on stage, delivering sonic excellence and good vibes wherever I can.

I pride myself on my ability to adapt, learn, and grow in pretty much every situation, but I specialize in mixing and producing alternative, pop, and rock music. I also moonlight as a front of house and playback engineer, and have worked on a few specialized recording studio builds as a consulting audio engineer.

My philosophy is not controversial: your music should sound great, whatever that means to you. I believe anyone can get to that point without breaking the budget or putting themselves in rooms with assholes. If that sounds like the right attitude, head over to the Credits & Services page to see if we’d be a good fit, and shoot me a message.
